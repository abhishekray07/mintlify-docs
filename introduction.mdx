## What is Opslane?

Opslane is an open-source AI On-Call Co-Pilot designed to make the on-call experience less stressful. It leverages AI to reduce alert fatigue, provide contextual information, and automate routine tasks.

## Why Opslane?

Most engineers don't enjoy being on-call. Opslane addresses common challenges:

* **Alert fatigue**: Reduce noisy alerts

* **Lack of context**: Understand root causes quickly

* **Manual incident resolution**: Automate routine tasks

* **Monitoring tool overload**: Integrate multiple tools

* **Burnout prevention**: Make on-call duties more manageable
